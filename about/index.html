About

Ahoy! My name is Marc and I am an engineering student from France. This place is where I write stuff about my daily geek reveries.

I have always loved learning/sharing about software and web development using websites like the Stack Exchange family, GitHub and various other personnal or business blogs.

I am currently working as a Grails developer for a french startup named kazaden, and I love words like ElasticSearch, Amazon Web Services, or Linux.

When I'm not busy trying to get my old laptop to compile and run my projects, I like to go out and take pictures, print them in my darkroom or read cool photography books. I also like enjoying a beer in front of a rugby game (Go Clermont) and listening to cool music.

Feel free to contact me on twitter if you want to chat about dev things and/or have a beer!
